where woke meets vogue

having trouble finding “new looks?”

experiencing existential dread when you see the back of your wardrobe?

wondering why what’s “fashionable” changes with the blink of an eye ?

don’t worry, we understand.

it’s hard to be fashionable, budget sensitive, & environmentally conscious all at once. some would say it’s nearly impossible. but that’s where we come in.

at ctrl-alt-thread, our mission is to help you refashion the threads you already have. so, what are you waiting for? get started below and explore our custom upcycling tool for reducing your fashion footprint & expenses.

Get Started

85% of textiles are sent to landfills.

But, 95% of discarded clothing can be recycled or upcycled.

our mission:

it’s simple. we make fashionable synonymous with environmentally & budget conscious.

our goals:

  • reduce textile waste creatively: help you reshape the threads you have
  • make upcycling effortless & collaborative
  • help you save your money & the environment

how it works :

  • enter the details of a garment you were planning to purchase. we’ll tell you how you can make it by upcycling materials you may already have lying around at home via a customized set of tutorials.
  • decide on a tutorial, & once you’ve made it, add it to your user portfolio
  • track your savings, environmental points, & upcycled creations in your personalized user dashboard
