Our Story

85% of textiles are sent to landfills.

But, 95% of discarded clothing can be recycled or upcycled.

we love fashion, but we’re also students who understand (all too well) the struggles associated with constantly updating your wardrobe. after discovering our shared love for the social media influencer, best dressed, we realized that updating your wardrobe doesn’t have to be hard but rather, a creative outlet & sustainable process. ctrl-alt-thread is grounded in the belief that true change lies in the confluence of small acts of activism. our upcycling tool contributes to the elimination of fashion waste, one thread at a time.
